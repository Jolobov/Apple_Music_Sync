## üç™ *Cookies*

### What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that contain your authentication data.
This program needs them to prove that you have an active Apple Music subscription and allows it to access the tracks for downloading.

### How to get them

You need to export your cookies in the **Netscape HTTP Cookie File** format from your browser after logging into Apple Music.

You can use these browser extensions:

- Firefox: [Export Cookies.txt](https://addons.mozilla.org/ru/firefox/addon/export-cookies-txt)
- Chrome/Edge: [Get cookies.txt LOCALLY](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/get-cookiestxt-locally/cclelndahbckbenkjhflpdbgdldlbecc)
